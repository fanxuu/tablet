Michigan Hillel reimbursement system

UM Hillel
User Research
Service Design
I worked with four other team members and provided the consulting sevice for Michigan Hillel in order to streamline its reimbursement system. The current process takes at least 4 weeks to finish one reimburesement. Several stakeholders have to check and sign on the request forms. Meanwhile, paper works get lost during the process. We conducted interviews, analyzed data and figured out solutions.

UX Design for Segway DeliveryBots

Segway-Ninebot
UX Deisgn
Product Management
I worked with my team and targeted the issue of delivering packages in office buildings. I was in charge of UX design and project management relevant to delivery robots and the robot station. I designed the workflow of robots, user flow, interaction, the monitoring platform, etc. The staff in the office reception room no longer need to run around the building all day and robots will do that. The most fun part was to figure out how people interact with robots.
